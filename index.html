Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe for solo female travelers?

Absolutely. Safety is our primary priority. We only deploy verified, background-checked local guides, maintain private campsite enclosures, and ensure communication lines are active at all times. Solo travelers are paired in group structures or offered dedicated escorts.

Are dietary requirements accommodated in the desert?

Yes, definitely. Our professional desert chefs are trained to prepare dedicated Vegan, Vegetarian, Gluten-Free, and Halal cuisines. Please make sure to select your dietary preferences during checkout so we can prepare fresh ingredients.

What is your cancellation and weather policy?

We offer full 100% refunds for cancellations made up to 48 hours before your booking date. If severe sandstorms occur, we offer a free rebooking window or instant full refunds.

What should I pack for an overnight stay?

Pack a warm jacket (desert nights can get cold, down to 10°C / 50°F), comfortable walking shoes, sunscreen, a hat, and personal toiletries. We provide premium bedding, sleeping bags, towels, and filtered water.
